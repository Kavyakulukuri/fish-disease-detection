📈 Fish Farming Growth Timeline

  • Pond Preparation: Cleaning, disinfecting, and adjusting pH levels.
  • Stocking the Fish: Selecting and acclimatizing healthy fingerlings.
  • Feeding Management: Providing balanced feed for optimal growth.
  • Water Quality Monitoring: Checking oxygen levels, ammonia, and pH balance.
  • Fish Growth Tracking: Measuring fish weight and size regularly.
  • Disease Prevention & Treatment: Identifying symptoms early and applying treatments.
  • Harvesting & Selling: Ensuring proper harvesting and market storage.
